# Initial Concept

Portfolio Prism is a privacy-first desktop portfolio analyzer that runs entirely on the user's machine, utilizing a Tauri shell with a Python sidecar for analytics.

# Product Guide

## Overview
A privacy-first desktop portfolio analyzer that runs entirely on the user's machine. It bridges the gap between complex manual spreadsheets and privacy-invasive cloud platforms.

## Core Purpose
To empower investors with deep, multi-dimensional analytics that extend beyond simple holdings. By providing a transparent view of the impact of every investment decision, Portfolio Prism enables users to exercise independent judgment and secure their financial future through data-driven confidence.

## Key Features
- **Local-First Analysis:** Data stays on the machine; privacy is the foundation of user trust.
- **Trade Republic Integration:** Automated sync via 2FA.
- **Community ISIN Resolution:** Crowdsourced mapping via "The Hive".
- **Offline Mode:** Full functionality with cached data.
- **Native Performance:** Lightweight Tauri shell.

## Target Users
- **Sovereign Investors:** Individuals who prioritize total control over their assets and are cautious of delegating trust, particularly concerning their pension and long-term financial security.
- **Analytical Decision-Makers:** Users who value transparency and seek rich, granular data to facilitate self-sufficient and informed financial choices.
- **Privacy Advocates:** Investors who refuse to compromise on data security and demand a high-performance, local-only native experience.
